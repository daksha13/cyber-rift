KEYBOARD

KEYBOARD FOR LAPTOPS ARE OF MAINLY 3 TYPES: TRADITIONAL STYLE,CHICLET STYLE,MECHANICAL STYLE.

TRADITIONAL STYLE

A TRADITIONAL STYLE KEYBORAD HAS KEY CLOSE TO ONE ANOTHER WITH EACH EDGE SLOPING OFF. IT GENERALY OFFERS MORE SURFACE AREA AND KEY TRAVEL AND IS EASIER TO REPLACE BECAUSE THE ENTIRE KEYBOARD SITS ON A REPLACEABLE TRAY

CHICLET STYLE

A CHICKET STYLE OR (ISLAND STYLE) KEYBOARD HAS KEYS THAT APPEAR TO POP OUT OF THE LAPTOP'S BODY THROUGH SEPERATE CUT-OUTS FOR EACH KEY . KEYS ARE USUALLY FLAT AND DON'T SLOPE OFF AROUND THE EDGES. SO IT CREATES AN IMPRESSION THAT THERE IS MORE SPACE BETWEEN THE KEYS . IT ALSO MAKES CLEANING THE KEYBOARD MUCH EASIER . THE CHICLET STYLE KEYBOARD IS GRADUALLY ECLIPSING THE TRADITIONAL KEYBOARD IN LAPTOPS TODAY IT'S THINNER AND MORE VERSATILE IN DESIGN ; SO IT'S BECOMING THE KEYBOARD OF CHOICE FOR THIN AND LIGHT LAPTOP MAKERS.BACKLIT KEYBOARD ARE MOSRE OFTEN CHICLET STYLE SO IF YOU ARE GOING FOR BACLIT CHOOSE THE CHICLET STYLE.

MECHANICAL STYLE

UNLIKE TRADITIONAL OR CHICLET STYLE KEYBOARD ,WHICH USES MEMBRANE UNDER THE KEYS , A MECHANICAL KEYBOARDS EMPLOYS REAL MECHANICAL UNDER THE KEYCAPS . THE SWITCHES PRODUCE AN AUDIBLE CLICK WITH EACH, MAKING FOR INCREASED FEEDBACK . A MECHANICAL KEYBOARD IS MORE COMMONLY FOUND IN DESKTOP THAN LAPTOPS BECAUSE IT USES MORE SPACE AND MORE EXPENSIVE DESIGN .THE KEYS ON A MECHANICAL KEYBOARD TRAVEL DEEP AND ARE TO LAST A VERY LONG TIME.



PRO-TIP:

CHANCES ARE YOU WILL COME ACROSS A CHICLET STYLE KEYBOARD ON YOUR NEXT LAPTOP , UNLESS YOU GO LOOKING SPECIFIFCALLY FOR A MECHANICAL OR TRADITIONALSTYLE KEYBOARD. IN SUCH A CASE YOU NEED TO ONLY OBSERVE THE KEYBOARD FOR TRAVEL, TACTILITY ,OVERALL COMFORTAND KEY FUNCTIONALITY . YOU CAN'T REALLY GO WRONG WITH ANY KEYBOARD ,BUT IF YOU FIND A KEYBOARD THAT SUITS YOUR TYPING STYLE ,IT COULD BOOST YOUR PRODUCTIVITY FIVEFOLD AND KEEP YOUR FINGERTIPS SMILLING.

HARD DISK AND STORAGE

INTERNAL STORAGE:


THIS IS ASLO REFERRED TO AS THE HARD DRIVE SIZE OR CAPACITY OF THE LAPTOP . STORAGE ON A LAPTOP IS WHERE THE OPERATING SYSTEMS (LIKE WINDOWS OR MAC OS) ARE INSTALLED .THIS IS ALSO WHERE ALL DOCUMENTS ,PHOTOS , APPS ,GAMES AND MOVIES ARE STORED .
WITHOUT SUFFICIENT STORAGE , YOU WILL NOT BE ABLE TO STORE ANYTHING , AND IT WILL ALSO IMPACT THE SPEED PERFORMANCE .
FOR THOSE ON A BUDGET ,HDDs ARE A BETTER OPTION WHILE THOSE INTO GAMING ARE BEST OFF CHOOSING AN SSD .



USUALLY , LAPTOP STORAGE IS OF 3 TYPES: HARD DISK DRIVE(HDD),SOLID STATE DRIVE (SSD) AND SOLID STATE HYBRID DRIVE(SSHD) :

INTERNAL MEMORY TYPES

HARD DISK DRIVESOLID STATE DRIVE SOLID STATE HYBRID DRIVE
A MECHANICAL HARD DISK DRIVE(OFTEN SHORTENED HDD) IS A TYPE OF SHORTAGE WHICH GIVES GIVES YOU LOTS OF CAPACITY ON A BUDGET ..

A HDD IS CONSIDERED SLOW BECAUSE IT HAS MOVING PARTS THAN SPIN TO READ DATA. 		A SOLID STATE DRIVE (SOMETIMES CALLED SSD)IS A TYPE OF DRIVE THAT IS FASTER , BUT ALSO MORE EXPENSIVE ,THAN HDDs
SSDs CAN START UP WINDOWS IN LESS THAN 5 SECONDS .

PROGRAMS WILL RUN FASTER IF YOU ARE A SSD.

HOWERVER ALL THIS PERFORMANCE COMES AT A HIGHER PRICE.		SSHD IS A COMBINATION OF HDD AND SSD WHICH NOT ONLY MEANS YOU GET STORAGE SPACE BUT EVERYTHING LOADS FASTER TOO

EXAMPLE: A SSHD UNIT COULD HAVE A 128GB SSD PAIRED WITH A 500 GB OR 1TB HDD .

THE OPERATING SYSTEM WILL BE INSTALLED ON THE SSD ALONG WITH OTHER PROGRAMS WHILE THE HDD PART IS USED TO STORE DOCUMENTS ,MOVIES ETC. THIS MEANS THAT YOUR OS AND PROGRAMS START UP FASTER.

SCREEN SIZE
